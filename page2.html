Amelito's Sizzling House

This Sizzling house is famous to all Maloleno's because of the cheap meals and the unli rice, gravy and soup they serve. 

This Sizzling House is famous to all Maloleno's with it's fast service and unlimited food it offers. Thanks to the Comfort food that never gets old and cheap prices they offer this place is a go to place of students from different schools.

Where?

From Bulacan State University (Main Campus) you'll ride a jeep going to Malolos Crossing. The location of the Sizzling house is across Puregold Jr. beside the Chooks-to-go.

Order?

From different sizzling dishes to pancits
