Blaire's Board and Bistro

If you are hungry and wants to waste time, then this is the place your looking for. Enjoy quality time with your friends and special someone while you eat, sing, play games and make memories at Blaire's...

Enjoy the mouthwatering food and savor the excitement of returning back to your childhood as you go to this place they offer different kinds of Board Games and meriendas which make it the best place to kill time while waiting for your next class.

Where?

From Bulacan State University (Main Campus). It is across the University Main Gate and inside the Eco-Commercial Building Extension.

Order?

Lunch meals, shakes and milk tea, and different merienda dishes
