Lugaw ni Abel

This may be a bit far from the BulSU campus but students went here because of their delicious homemade porridge. It is famous even in summer.

Experience the visually flavorful foods as you enter to the aesthetic design and instagram worthy foods. The right diner for snack lovers and people who love to take selfies.

Where?

This porridge place is beside the Santissima Trinidad Church.

Order?

Sizzlings, porridge, and filipino 'kakanin'
